My Favourite Food

My favourite food is spaghetti and fish. It is easy to prepare. It is also very delicious.

Ingredients for 2 people

  • Spaghetti(1)
  • blended tomato and onions
  • Carrot
  • fish
  • spices

Steps to making spaghetti for 2 persons

  1. Break the spaghetti and boil it for 8 to 10 minutes
  2. blend tomato and onions together.
  3. Then just add all the other ingredients I mentioned. Then you have your spaghetti or like Italians call it pasta
